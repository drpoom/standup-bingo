# SK Hynix Financials & HBM Position

**Source date:** Q1 2025 results (Apr 24, 2025); market outlook Jan 2026

## Q1 2025 Key Financials (K-IFRS)

| Metric | Q1 2025 | QoQ | YoY |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue | ₩17.64T (~$12.4B) | -11% | +42% |
| Operating Profit | ₩7.44T | -8% | +158% |
| Operating Margin | 42% | +1pp | +19pp |
| Net Income | ₩8.11T | +1% | +323% |
| Net Margin | 46% | — | — |

- Revenue & operating profit: 2nd-highest quarterly records (after Q4 2024 peak)
- Operating margin improved for 8th consecutive quarter
- Net income beat consensus (₩5.48T) by ~48%
- Cash & equivalents: ₩14.3T; debt ratio 29%, net debt ratio 11%

## HBM Market Share

- **~62%** of HBM shipments (Q2 2025, Counterpoint Research)
- **~57%** of HBM revenue (Q3 2025, Counterpoint Research)
- Dominant supplier of HBM3E 12-layer to Nvidia
- 2025 HBM sales expected to **double** vs 2024; 2025 production sold out
- 12-layer HBM3E to exceed 50% of total HBM3E revenue in Q2 2025

## Guidance & Outlook

- HBM demand to approximately double in 2025 vs 2024 (maintained)
- HBM4 mass production prep targeted by end-2025
- HBM3E to remain flagship product through 2026 (~⅔ of HBM shipments)
- BofA forecasts 2026 HBM market at $54.6B (+58% YoY); named SK Hynix "Top Pick"
- Capex discipline: focus on demand-feasible, profitable products
- eSSD demand rising; NAND managed with profitability-first approach

## Key Risk

- US tariff uncertainty (25% on Korean imports, 90-day pause; semiconductors currently exempt but new chip tariffs expected)
