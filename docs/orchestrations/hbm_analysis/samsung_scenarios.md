## Methodology Notes

1. **HBM revenue attribution**: Samsung HBM revenue estimated from projected market share × broad-definition HBM TAM (Yole/TrendForce methodology). Samsung's ~17% share (mid-2025) is the starting point.
2. **Foundry drag**: Based on Samsung Foundry's persistent losses (estimated ₩3–5T annual loss in 2025). 2nm GAA ramp is the key inflection; TSMC's 2nm progress is the competitive benchmark.
3. **FX assumption**: ₩1,430/USD (2025 average); scenarios use ₩1,400–1,450 range.
4. **Share count**: 5.96B shares outstanding (Samsung Electronics common + preferred, as of FY 2025).
5. **DS margin decomposition**: Memory margins estimated at 35–45% (upcycle) to 15–25% (downcycle); Foundry margins at –15% (bear) to +8% (bull); System LSI at 2–5%.

*Sources: Samsung FY 2025 earnings releases, Counterpoint Research, TrendForce, Yole Group, Blocks & Files, CNBC, DigitalToday*
