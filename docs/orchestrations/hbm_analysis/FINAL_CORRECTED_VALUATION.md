## 6. WHY THE ORIGINAL ERROR OCCURRED

The original "10.8×" FY2028 P/E for SK Hynix was caused by one or more of:
1. **Using stale FY2028 EPS estimates** from before the HBM ramp (when FY2028 EPS was projected much lower)
2. **Mixing per-share and aggregate figures** (e.g., dividing price by net income in billions instead of per-share EPS)
3. **Using a different/wrong share count** for different years

The consensus now shows SK Hynix FY2028 net income at ₩266T — essentially flat vs FY2027's ₩266T. This means FY2028 EPS ≈ FY2027 EPS, so P/E stays around 4.8× rather than declining further. Revenue still grows (₩475T vs ₩445T) but margins compress from ~77% to ~74%.
