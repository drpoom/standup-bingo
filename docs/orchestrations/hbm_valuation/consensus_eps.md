## Methodology Notes

1. **SK Hynix & Samsung EPS**: Computed as Net Income ÷ Shares Outstanding since direct per-share consensus estimates are less widely published for Korean-listed stocks. Shares outstanding sourced from companiesmarketcap.com (SK Hynix: 690.4M) and stockanalysis.com (Samsung: 6.65B).

2. **Micron EPS**: Directly available from multiple US-based financial data providers. MarketScreener and StockAnalysis figures are most consistent. MarketWatch figures for FY2026/2027 appear to use a different accounting basis and are noted as outliers.

3. **Fiscal Year Alignment**: 
   - SK Hynix: FY ends Dec 31
   - Samsung: FY ends Dec 31
   - Micron: FY ends late Aug/early Sep (FY2026 = Sep 2025–Aug 2026)

4. **Data freshness**: All estimates retrieved on 2026-05-16. Consensus figures are dynamic and subject to revision.
