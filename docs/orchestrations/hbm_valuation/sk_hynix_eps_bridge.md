| **Blended GM%** | 54.2% | 64.8% | 63.1% | 61.3% |
| SGA&D (₩T) | 4.9 | 7.2 | 10.8 | 13.5 |
| SGA&D % of Revenue | 5.0% | 3.5% | 4.0% | 4.5% |
| **EBITDA (₩T)** | **57.5** | **140.1** | **181.2** | **197.4** |
| **EBITDA Margin** | **59.2%** | **68.3%** | **67.1%** | **65.8%** |

> **SGA&D:** FY2025A at 5.0% of revenue. FY2026E compresses to 3.5% on operating leverage (revenue doubles, fixed cost base grows modestly). Normalizes to 4.0–4.5% by FY2028E as revenue growth slows and SG&A investments in HBM4/HBM4E R&D increase.

> **EBITDA definition:** Gross Profit + SGA&D (i.e., revenue minus COGS, before D&A). This is a gross EBITDA measure. Some definitions subtract SGA&D; we add it back since SGA&D is an operating cost already subtracted from gross profit to arrive at operating income.

SK Hynix guides capex at mid-30% of revenue. D&A ramps from 1.5% of revenue (FY2026E) to 5.0% (FY2028E) as new assets are placed in service.

### Key Differences Explained

1. **Revenue:** Our model uses capacity-model-derived organic revenue (₩205T/₩270T/₩300T) vs. consensus (₩332T/₩445T/₩476T). The consensus figures likely include Solidigm consolidation (acquired FY2025) and may reflect different HBM ASP assumptions. Our model is deliberately conservative.

2. **Share Count:** Consensus uses 690.4M shares (pre-cancellation). Our model accounts for the Jan 2026 cancellation of 15.3M shares and forward buyback guidance (~1.5–2% annual reduction).

3. **Tax Rate:** We use 25% normalized. Consensus may incorporate Korean tax incentives for semiconductor investment (effective rates can be 20–23%).

4. **Other Income:** We model conservatively (₩5T/₩3T/₩2T) vs. the Q1'26 run-rate which included ₩9.9T in Kioxia valuation gains.

## 10. Key Assumptions & Risks

### Assumptions
1. **Revenue:** Based on capacity model (HBM wafer input, ASP/GB, mix ratios). See `sk_hynix_capacity.md`.
2. **Margins:** Based on product-mix margin model (HBM GM 70–73%, Commodity DRAM GM 57–65%, NAND GM 50–55%). See `sk_hynix_margin.md`.
3. **D&A:** Ramps from 1.5% to 5.0% of revenue as M15X, Yongin Phase-1, and Indiana fab assets are placed in service.
4. **Tax Rate:** 25% normalized (Korean corporate tax including local surtaxes for large corporations).
5. **Share Count:** Declining from 675M → 639M over 3 years (~2% annual reduction via buybacks/cancellations).
6. **Other Income:** Conservative; excludes one-time valuation gains.

### Key Risks
1. **HBM pricing correction:** A 10% HBM ASP decline reduces HBM GM by ~5pp, reducing FY2026E EPS by ~₩15,000.
2. **Samsung HBM4 qualification:** If Samsung gains NVIDIA Rubin share, SK Hynix HBM revenue share could drop from 57% to 45%.
3. **KRW/USD volatility:** 10% KRW appreciation reduces reported margins by ~2–3pp.
4. **CapEx overruns:** Yongin Phase-1 (₩160T total) could accelerate D&A beyond modeled levels.
5. **Share count uncertainty:** Buyback pace depends on net cash generation and ADR listing decisions.
