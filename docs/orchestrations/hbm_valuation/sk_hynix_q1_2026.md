| **Net Margin** | 77% | — | — | — | — |

> **Note on Net Income**: KRW 40.3459T includes KRW 9.94T in valuation gains on investment assets (primarily Kioxia-related) and KRW 1.57T in FX gains. Stripped of non-operating items, the clean operating profit baseline is KRW 37.61T (~$27B).

## HBM Revenue Share

| Metric | Q1 2026 | Q4 2025 |
|---|---|---|
| **HBM as % of DRAM revenue** | **~12%** | ~6% |
| **DRAM as % of total revenue** | 78% (~KRW 41T) | — |
| **NAND as % of total revenue** | 21% (~KRW 11T) | — |
| **Implied HBM revenue** | **~KRW 4.9T (~$3.5B)** | — |

- HBM revenue share of DRAM doubled from 6% to 12% QoQ
- DRAM bit shipments were flat QoQ; DRAM ASP rose ~mid-60% QoQ (mix-driven, not volume-driven)
- NAND bit shipments fell ~10% QoQ; NAND ASP rose ~mid-70% QoQ
- Management stated HBM demand exceeds supply capacity for the next 3+ years

## Capital Expenditure (CapEx)

| Metric | Detail |
|---|---|
| **FY2026 CapEx guidance** | **Mid-30% of revenue** (~KRW 18–20T annualized based on current run-rate) |
| **Q1 2026 CapEx** | Not separately disclosed in quarterly results; company stated "investment scale will increase significantly compared to the previous year" |

### Balance Sheet Context
- Cash & equivalents: KRW 54.3T (~$39B), up KRW 19.4T QoQ
- Interest-bearing debt: KRW 19.3T, down KRW 2.9T QoQ
- **Net cash position: KRW 35T (~$25B)** — flipped from KRW 9T net debt in just two quarters
- Management targeting >KRW 100T net cash position

---

## Key Business Highlights

- **Record quarter**: First time quarterly revenue exceeded KRW 50T; operating margin at all-time high of 72%
- **HBM4**: Under development with key customers; production ramp per agreed schedules
- **HBM4E**: Samples planned H2 2026; mass production targeted 2027; core die on 1c nm process
- **SOCAMM2**: 192GB module for NVIDIA Vera Rubin platform entered mass production April 2026
- **LPDDR6**: Industry's first 1c nm LPDDR6; full-scale supply planned H2 2026
- **321-layer QLC NAND**: PQC21 client SSD shipping; >50% of domestic NAND production to migrate to 321-layer by year-end
- **Q2 2026 Guidance**: DRAM bit growth +7–9% QoQ; NAND bit growth +16–17% QoQ (includes Solidigm consolidation effect)
- **U.S. ADR listing**: Confidential SEC filing made March 24, 2026; targeting listing within 2026
