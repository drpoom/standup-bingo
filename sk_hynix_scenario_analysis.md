## Scenario Definitions

### 🟢 Bull Case (35% Probability): "AI Memory Supercycle Accelerates"

**Core Thesis:** SK Hynix extends HBM dominance beyond 60%, SoW-X (Silicon Wafer with Extreme) adoption accelerates with NVIDIA Rubin/Custom ASICs, and margin expansion continues through 2028 as supply remains structurally constrained.

**Key Assumptions:**
| Factor | 2026 | 2027 | 2028 |
|---|---|---|---|
| HBM Market Share | 62% | 63% | 60% |
| HBM Market Size ($B) | $54.6 | $78 | $105 |
| DRAM ASP Growth | +33% | +15% | +8% |
| NAND ASP Growth | +26% | +12% | +5% |
| OP Margin | 62–65% | 58–60% | 52–55% |
| SoW-X/Custom HBM | Early ramp (5% of HBM) | 15% of HBM | 25% of HBM |
| Capex/Revenue | ~22% | ~25% | ~28% |

**Rationale:**
- UBS projects ~70% HBM4 share for NVIDIA Rubin — SK Hynix captures disproportionate share
- SoW-X (advanced packaging integrating logic+memory on single wafer) creates new TAM and pricing tier
- Yongin cluster not online until H1 2028 → structural supply shortage through 2027
- Korean HBM4 suppliers (SK Hynix + Samsung) to supply ~90% of global HBM4 demand
- Custom HBM for Google TPU, AWS Trainium, and other ASICs expands addressable market
- HBM4E (7th gen) sampling H2 2026 cements technology leadership

## Summary Assessment

SK Hynix is at an inflection point where Q1 2026 margins (72% OP) likely represent a cyclical peak. The key question is **how much margin normalizes** and **whether HBM share erosion is gradual or sharp**:

- **Bull case** assumes structural supply shortage persists through 2027, SoW-X creates new premium tier, and SK Hynix maintains >60% HBM share. Implies stock upside to ~₩2.7M+.
- **Base case** assumes healthy but moderating cycle — margins compress from 72% toward 50% by 2027, HBM share gradually declines to ~53%. Still implies upside to ~₩2.1M at 20x EPS.
- **Bear case** assumes competitive erosion + cyclical downturn hits 2027–28, margins compress to mid-20s%. Implies downside to ~₩800K–1.0M.

**The probability-weighted expected EPS of ~₩112,500 for 2026** suggests the stock at ₩1,970,000 trades at ~17.5x weighted 2026 EPS — reasonable for a cyclical peak but with significant upside if the bull case materializes.
