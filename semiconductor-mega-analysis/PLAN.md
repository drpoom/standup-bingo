## 8. Dhandho Screening Criteria (Preview)

For Phase 7A, the screening will apply:

| Criterion | Metric | Threshold |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Low Valuation | EV/FCF vs 5-year avg | ≤ 0.8× (20%+ discount) |
| High Growth | Revenue CAGR 2024-2026E | ≥ 15% |
| Misunderstood Cyclicality | Cyclical positioning | Near trough OR structural inflection |
| Margin of Safety | P/E vs normalized EPS | ≤ 0.75× (25%+ discount) |
| Balance Sheet | Net Debt/EBITDA | ≤ 2.0× |
| Moat | Switching costs / scale / IP | Score ≥ 3/5 |

**Expected Dhandho Candidates (hypothesis):**
- Memory stocks (MU, SK Hynix) — cyclical trough + HBM structural shift
- Equipment stocks (LRCX, AMAT, KLAC) — cyclical trough + EUV/advanced node demand
- Materials stocks (Shin-Etsu, JSR) — overlooked chokepoints with pricing power
- Analog (TXN, ADI) — misunderstood cyclicality in auto/industrial recovery
