# Top 30 Selection Criteria

> **Created:** 2026-05-10

## Screening Methodology

### Step 1: Universe Filter (~80 → ~45)
- Market Cap ≥ $10B (or regional significance)
- Revenue ≥ $3B (or high growth trajectory)
- Liquid, investable (excludes micro-caps, delisted, private)

### Step 2: Composite Scoring (~45 → 30)

| Factor | Weight | Scale | Description |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Moat | 30% | 1-5 | Switching costs, scale, IP, network effects |
| Strategic Importance | 25% | 1-5 | Chokepoint power, monopoly/duopoly position |
| Growth | 20% | 1-5 | Revenue CAGR 2024-2026E |
| Size/Liquidity | 15% | 1-5 | Market cap, trading volume, investability |
| Cyclicality Opportunity | 10% | 1-5 | Near trough OR structural inflection |

### Exclusions Applied
- **MRAM** ($0.5B) — Below market cap threshold
- **ALTR** (Altera) — Not publicly traded
- **SILC** (Siltec) — Does not exist
- **4183.T** (JSR) — Delisted
- **All China A-shares** — Geopolitical risk, limited ADR access
- **SIE** (Siemens) — <2% semiconductor revenue
- **AMS-Osram** ($1.1B) — Below threshold, debt burden

### Dhandho Overlay
The cyclicality factor specifically identifies stocks near cyclical troughs or structural inflections — key for Dhandho investing (low valuation + misunderstood cyclicality).
