## What the Market is Missing

1. **SK Hynix:** HBM is structural, not cyclical — 2-3 year packaging moat insulates pricing
2. **Samsung:** Foundry optionality is a free call option; Korean discount is permanent but too wide
3. **STMicro:** Priced for permanent depression at auto trough; SiC ramp is structural
4. **Renesas:** 50-70% discount to auto-semi peers is unjustified for #1 MCU supplier
5. **Shin-Etsu:** Wafer supply is the most concentrated chokepoint in semis; AI chiplets use MORE wafer area
