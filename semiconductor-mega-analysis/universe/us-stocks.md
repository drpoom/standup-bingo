## Key Findings & Corrections

### Ticker/Exchange Corrections
- **COHR**: Listed on **NYSE**, not NASDAQ. Corrected.
- **ALTR**: The ticker **ALTR now belongs to Altair Engineering** (a simulation software company, NASDAQ: ALTR). The original Altera Corporation was acquired by Intel in 2015 and delisted. The new standalone Altera (spun from Intel PSG) is **not yet publicly traded** — Silver Lake acquired 51% in April 2025. No public ticker exists.

### Sub-Sector Refinements
- **AVGO**: Broadened from "Networking" to "Networking/AI" — Broadcom is now a major AI custom silicon (ASIC) and networking player.
- **QCOM**: Broadened to "Mobile/AI" — Qualcomm expanding AI capabilities.
- **AMAT**: Broadened from "Deposition" to "Deposition/Process" — AMAT covers more than just deposition.
- **MPWR**: Clarified as "Analog (Power Management)" rather than just "Analog (Power)".
- **NXPI**: Clarified as "Analog (Auto/Industrial)" — NXP's primary strength.
- **WDC**: Updated to "Memory (NAND/HDD)" — WDC is now primarily HDD-focused with NAND via Kioxia JV.
- **COHR**: Clarified as "Equipment (Laser/Photonics)" — more specific than just "Laser".

### Stocks to Consider Removing
- **ALTR (Altera)**: Not publicly traded. Should be removed or flagged as private/pre-IPO.
