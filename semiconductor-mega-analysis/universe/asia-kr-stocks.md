## Notes

- **Samsung Electronics (005930.KS):** The dominant Korean semiconductor play. Memory (DRAM/NAND) is the core profit driver, but also has growing foundry and system LSI businesses. Market cap has fluctuated significantly with memory cycle.
- **SK Hynix (000660.KS):** Surged on HBM3E demand for AI accelerators. Market cap briefly topped $150B+ in 2025. The preferred shares (036490.KS) trade at a slight discount.
- **HANMI Semiconductor (042700.KS):** Not to be confused with Hanmi Pharm. Major test handler equipment supplier, benefiting from advanced packaging demand.
- **HPSP (098460.KS):** Relatively new listing; specializes in spatial ALD equipment for high-k metal gate processes. Small but growing.
