## Notes on Original List Corrections

- **KYEC (5274.TW → 3244.TW):** The original ticker 5274 points to ASPEED Technology, a BMC (Baseboard Management Controller) chip design company on TPEX. The correct ticker for KYEC (King Yuan Electronics Corp., IC testing) is 3244.TW on TWSE.
- **King Yuan Electronics (3131.TW → 2449.TW):** 3131 is Grand Process Technology on TPEX (OTC market), a small process equipment maker. The correct ticker for King Yuan Electronics is 2449.TW. Note: 3244.TW and 2449.TW both refer to KYEC group entities.
- **Phihong (4966.TW):** 4966 is Parade Technologies on TPEX, an IC design company (display/USB interface chips). Phihong Technology (2457.TW) makes power supplies and is not a semiconductor materials company. This entry should be reconsidered.
