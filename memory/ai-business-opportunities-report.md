## Executive Summary

**3 Key Insights:**

1. **95% of small business AI projects fail** before producing value—not because AI doesn't work, but because integration fails. This is your exact skill set.

2. **The "AI plumber" gap is real and growing:** Companies have AI tools but can't connect them to actual workflows. They'll pay someone to make it work, not to sell them another tool.

3. **Low-competition niches exist where AI does the work but humans must orchestrate:** Compliance documentation, industry-specific workflow integration, and "AI rescue" services for failed implementations.

**Bottom line:** Your system integration expertise is the scarce resource. AI is commoditized. Integration is not.

### Opportunity #3: Vertical AI Workflow Templates (Micro-SaaS)

**The Problem:**  
Generic AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) don't understand specific industry workflows. A plumber, a dentist, and a real estate agent all need different AI workflows—but no one is building industry-specific templates with integrated tools.

**The Solution:**  
Build **industry-specific AI workflow packages** - pre-built, documented, ready-to-deploy:
- **Dental Practice AI:** Patient communication, appointment reminders, treatment plan explanations, insurance documentation
- **Real Estate AI:** Listing descriptions, buyer qualification, document preparation, market analysis reports
- **Legal Small Practice AI:** Client intake, document drafting, case research summaries, billing time tracking

Each package includes:
- Prompt templates (customized for the industry)
- Integration guides (connect to their existing tools)
- Video tutorials
- 30-day support

**Why Now:**  
- AI tools are commoditized (everyone has access)
- **Implementation is the barrier**—people don't know how to apply AI to their specific work
- Vertical focus = less competition than generic "AI templates"
- Can start with one industry, expand based on demand

**Target Customer:**  
- Specific industry professionals (start with one)
- Solo practitioners or small firms (1-10 employees)
- Tech-comfortable but time-constrained
- Willing to pay €500-1,500 for "done for you" setup

**MVP Scope (Week 1):**
- Pick ONE industry (recommend: real estate or legal—high documentation, clear workflows)
- Interview 3-5 practitioners about their daily tasks
- Build workflow templates for top 5 use cases
- Create simple Notion page or Gumroad listing
- Price at €497-997 for early adopters
- Post in industry-specific forums, Facebook groups

**Revenue Model:**  
- One-time purchase: €497-1,497 per industry package
- Upsell: Customization service (€1,000-3,000)
- Upsell: Monthly support retainer (€200-500/month)
- Target: 10 sales/month = €5,000-15,000/month (scalable, no time trade-off)

**Competition:**  
- Generic prompt marketplaces (too broad, not industry-specific)
- Industry software (expensive, not AI-focused)
- **Your edge:** Deep workflow understanding + integration expertise + system thinking

**Risk Level:** **Medium**  
- Need to learn industry specifics (but this is defensible)
- Marketing challenge (finding the right channels)
- AI tools evolve (templates need updates)
- But: Once built, highly scalable

**Effort to Start:** **7-10 days**  
- Days 1-2: Pick industry, research, interview practitioners
- Days 3-5: Build workflow templates, test thoroughly
- Days 6-7: Create documentation, videos
- Days 8-9: Build landing page, set up payment
- Day 10: Launch, start outreach
