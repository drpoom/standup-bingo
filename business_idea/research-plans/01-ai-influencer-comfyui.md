## Mission

Research the feasibility of building an AI-generated influencer business using ComfyUI cloud infrastructure, focusing on:
1. Technical stack for consistent character generation
2. Monetization models (sponsorships, subscriptions, affiliate)
3. Specific concepts: Funny investment dog (Shiba/Cramer-style), Thai expat in Germany
4. Competitive landscape and differentiation
