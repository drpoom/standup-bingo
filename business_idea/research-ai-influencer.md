## 🇩🇪 German Legal Setup

### Business Registration

**Gewerbeanmeldung (Berlin):**
- **Cost:** €26 online
- **Process:** gewerbemeldung.berlin.de
- **Time:** 1-2 weeks processing
- **Required:** ID, address registration

### Tax Setup

**Kleinunternehmerregelung 2025:**
- **Threshold:** <€22K/year revenue
- **Benefit:** No VAT charging required
- **Paperwork:** Simplified tax filing
- **Upgrade:** Regular VAT if exceeding €22K

**Steuernummer (Tax ID):**
- **Apply:** After Gewerbeanmeldung
- **Processing:** 2-4 weeks
- **Form:** Fragebogen zur steuerlichen Erfassung

### Influencer Compliance

**Required Disclosures:**
1. **AI Disclosure:** "AI-generated content" (German law 2025)
2. **Affiliate Disclosure:** "#Werbung" or "#Anzeige"
3. **Sponsored Posts:** Clear brand partnership labels

**Legal Risk:** Low if compliant with disclosure rules
