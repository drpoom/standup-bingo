### 2.2 Content Production

**Workflow for Meme Videos (15-60 seconds):**

1. **Script Writing (10-15 min):**
   - Pick trending stock/crypto topic
   - Write Cramer-style rant ("This is a BUY!")
   - Add self-aware disclaimer ("I'm a dog, this is not advice")

2. **Image Generation (15-20 min):**
   - Generate 10-20 frames with Shiba character
   - Key expressions: excited, angry, pointing, chart-watching
   - Use IPAdapter for consistent poses

3. **Video Assembly (15-20 min):**
   - Interpolate frames (smooth motion)
   - Add captions (bold, meme-style fonts)
   - Add stock charts/screenshots
   - Add sound effects (Cramer "MMMMAAAARKET!", dog barks)

4. **Voiceover (5-10 min):**
   - TTS with Cramer-style voice (ElevenLabs or similar)
   - Or record own voice + pitch shift
   - Add dog bark sound effects for emphasis

5. **Total Time:** 45-65 minutes per video

**Daily Output:**
- **1 hour/day:** 1 short video OR 2-3 static memes
- **2 hours/day:** 2 short videos OR 1 long video
- **Batching:** Generate 20-30 images in one session, edit later

### 2.3 Automation Potential

**Can Be Automated (60-70%):**

- ✅ Caption generation (AI writes meme text)

## 4. Legal & Compliance

### 4.1 Regulatory Requirements

**SEC (US) / BaFin (Germany) Considerations:**

**Investment Advice Regulations:**
- **Investment Advice:** Requires license (Finanzanlagenberater in Germany)
- **Parody/Entertainment:** Generally exempt if clearly labeled as entertainment
- **Key Protection:** "Not Financial Advice" (NFA) disclaimers

**Required Disclaimers:**
- **Every video:** "Not financial advice. For entertainment only. I'm a dog."
- **Bio/Profile:** "Parody account. Not a financial advisor. Do your own research."
- **Affiliate posts:** "This is an affiliate link. I get paid if you sign up."
- **Sponsorships:** "Paid partnership with [Brand]."

**Legal Precedents:**
- **Parody Protection:** Strong in US (First Amendment) and EU (freedom of expression)
- **Disclosure Requirements:** Must be clear and conspicuous
- **No Specific Cases:** No known lawsuits against finance meme/parody accounts (as of 2025)

**BaFin Warnings (Germany):**
- BaFin actively warns about crypto/meme coin scams
- They target fraudulent schemes, not parody content
- **Risk Level:** Low if clearly labeled as entertainment

### 4.2 Platform Policies

| Platform | Finance Content Allowed? | AI Disclosure? | Monetization? | Notes |
|----------|-------------------------|----------------|---------------|-------|
| **YouTube** | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes (disclose in settings) | ✅ Yes | demonetization risk for "get rich quick" claims |
| **TikTok** | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes (AI label feature) | ✅ Yes | Finance content allowed, no guaranteed returns |
| **Instagram** | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | Must disclose paid partnerships |
| **Twitter/X** | ✅ Yes | ❌ No specific policy | ✅ Yes (ad revenue share) | Very permissive |

**Platform Risks:**
- **YouTube:** May demonetize videos with "get rich quick" language
- **TikTok:** May limit reach for finance content (shadowban risk)
- **All platforms:** Affiliate links may be flagged (use link-in-bio tools)

### 4.3 Compliance Checklist

**Before Launch:**
- [ ] Add disclaimer to bio: "Not financial advice. Entertainment only. I'm a dog."
- [ ] Create disclaimer template for video descriptions
- [ ] Set up business entity (Einzelunternehmer or UG)

**For Each Video:**
- [ ] Add "Not Financial Advice" text overlay (first 3 seconds)
- [ ] Include disclaimer in description

**Ongoing:**
