## Research Mission

Conduct comprehensive market research on AI-powered lead generation services to validate the business opportunity and identify optimal positioning.

---

## Phase 1: Competitor Landscape (8 minutes)

### Task 1.1: Identify Top Competitors (3 min)

**Search Queries:**
1. "AI lead generation services 2026"
2. "B2B lead generation agencies pricing"
3. "automated lead generation companies"
4. "AI-powered outbound sales agencies"

**Deliverable:** List of 10-15 competitors with URLs

---

### Task 1.2: Competitor Pricing Analysis (5 min)

**Fetch pricing from 5-7 competitors:**
- Apollo.io
- CIENCE
- Belkins
- LeadGenius
- Martal Group
- Artisan.co
- Mails.ai
- (Any others found in 1.1)

**Extract for each:**
- Pricing model (retainer, per-lead, subscription, hybrid)
- Specific prices (€, $ per month or per unit)
- Minimum commitment
- What's included at each tier
- Performance guarantees (if any)

**Deliverable:** Pricing comparison table

---

## Phase 2: Customer Demand Validation (7 minutes)

### Task 2.1: Customer Pain Points (3 min)

**Search Queries:**
1. "lead generation challenges B2B 2026"
2. "why lead generation fails Reddit"
3. "B2B sales pipeline problems"
4. "lead generation services reviews complaints"

**Extract:**
- Top 5 pain points customers mention
- What they dislike about current providers
- What they wish existed

**Deliverable:** Pain point summary with quotes

---

### Task 2.2: Willingness to Pay (4 min)

**Search Queries:**
1. "how much do companies pay for lead generation"
2. "B2B lead generation budget 2026"
3. "cost per qualified meeting B2B"
4. "lead generation ROI expectations"

**Extract:**
- Typical budgets (by company size)
- What they consider "expensive" vs "cheap"
- ROI expectations (what makes it "worth it")
- Decision criteria for choosing providers

**Deliverable:** Budget/ROI expectations summary

---

## Phase 3: Technical Feasibility (5 minutes)

### Task 3.1: AI Agent Capabilities (3 min)

**Search Queries:**
1. "AI agents for cold email outreach 2026"
2. "automated B2B prospecting tools"
3. "AI sales development representative"
4. "multi-agent sales automation"

**Extract:**
- What AI agents can do autonomously
- Where human oversight is still needed
- Best practices for AI outreach (deliverability, personalization)
- Tools/platforms that enable AI agents

**Deliverable:** AI capability assessment

---

### Task 3.2: Tech Stack Research (2 min)

**Fetch from 3-4 sources:**
- Best tools for AI lead generation
- Email infrastructure requirements
- Data sources (Apollo, ZoomInfo, etc.)
- Compliance considerations (GDPR, CAN-SPAM)

**Extract:**
- Required tools + costs
- Email deliverability best practices
- Compliance requirements for EU market

**Deliverable:** Tech stack + cost breakdown

---

## Phase 4: Market Sizing & Trends (3 minutes)

### Task 4.1: Market Size & Growth (2 min)

**Search Queries:**
1. "lead generation market size 2026"
2. "B2B sales automation market growth"
3. "AI in sales market forecast"

**Extract:**
- Total addressable market (TAM)
- Growth rate (CAGR)
- Key trends driving growth

**Deliverable:** Market size summary

---

### Task 4.2: Emerging Trends (1 min)

**Search Queries:**
1. "lead generation trends 2026"
2. "future of B2B sales AI"
3. "what's next in sales automation"

**Extract:**
- 3-5 emerging trends
- Opportunities these create
- Threats to traditional models

**Deliverable:** Trends summary

---

## Phase 5: Synthesis & Recommendations (2 minutes)

### Task 5.1: SWOT Analysis (1 min)

**Create:**
- **Strengths:** What advantages would an AI-agent-run service have?
- **Weaknesses:** What are the inherent limitations?
- **Opportunities:** Market gaps identified
- **Threats:** Competitive risks, market changes

---

### Task 5.2: Final Recommendations (1 min)

**Answer:**
1. **Is this opportunity viable?** (Yes/No/Maybe + why)
2. **Optimal positioning:** Price point, target customer, value prop
3. **Top 3 risks** to validate before starting
4. **Recommended next steps** (specific actions)
