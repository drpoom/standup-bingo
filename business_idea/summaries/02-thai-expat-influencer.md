## 🎯 Executive Summary

Create an AI-generated Thai expat influencer sharing life in Germany. Monetize through affiliate marketing, brand deals, and AdSense.

**Why it works:**
- Underserved niche (German-Thai expat content)
- Clear monetization: affiliate (5-15%), brand deals (€500-5K/post)
- Leverages Uncle John's authentic expat experience
- AI handles 70-80% of content creation
- Low startup cost (€30-50/mo)

---

## 📊 Market Analysis

**Target Audience:**
- Thai expats in Germany (~50K+ community)
- Germans interested in Thailand
- Global expats (relatable content)

**Content Themes:**
- German bureaucracy (visas, registration, taxes)
- Cultural differences (food, customs, language)
- Daily life hacks (shopping, healthcare, transport)
- Travel Germany ↔ Thailand

## 🤖 Technical Stack

**Infrastructure:**
- GPU Hosting: RunPod or Vast.ai (RTX 4090)
- Cost: €30-50/mo (hobby), €100-300/mo (production)

**Tools:**
- ComfyUI: Main generation workflow
- LoRA training: Character consistency
- IPAdapter: Style transfer
- Upscaling: 4x upscalers

**Time Investment:**
- Images: 2-5 min each
- Short videos: 15-45 min each
- Daily: 1-2 hours
