## Moat Score: 4
- #1 power semiconductor globally; #1 automotive MCU; SiC vertical integration (from substrate to module); ESMC JV with TSMC/Bosch/NXP secures EU fab capacity; deep automotive qualification moat (3-5 year cycles)

## Key Suppliers
- Foundry: Own fabs (Dresden, Villach, Kulim) + GlobalFoundries (long-term agreement) + TSMC (ESMC JV, JASM)
- Equipment: AMAT, ASML, LRCX, TEL, KLA
- Materials: Siltronic/Wacker (primary wafer supplier), SUMCO, Shin-Etsu, JSR/TOK, BASF/Merck, Linde/Air Liquide
- OSAT: ASE (strategic — sold Cavite/Cheonan sites to ASE in 2024), Amkor (Porto, Portugal facility)

## Dhandho Signal
- Reasonable EV/EBITDA 18 vs. analog peers; but EV/FCF 164 reflects heavy capex cycle (SiC ramp, ESMC fab); auto cyclical downturn is near-term headwind; SiC leadership and EU strategic value (CHIPS Act, ESMC) provide long-term floor; buy when auto cycle bottoms
