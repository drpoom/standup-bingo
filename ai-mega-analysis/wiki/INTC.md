## Moat Score: 2
- x86 architecture legacy provides installed base but eroding vs. AMD; foundry business has accumulated ~$30B+ in operating losses with limited external customers; process technology execution has been poor (repeated delays); High-NA EUV lead adoption is a bet, not a moat. IDM model is a potential advantage if executed, but execution risk is extreme.

## Key Suppliers
- Foundry: In-house (Oregon, Arizona, New Mexico, Ireland, Israel; Ohio & Germany under construction); also uses TSMC for competitive tiles
- Equipment: ASML (first High-NA EUV customer), Applied Materials, Lam Research, TEL, KLA, Carl Zeiss
- Materials: SUMCO, Shin-Etsu, Siltronic, GlobalWafers (wafers); JSR, TOK, Fujifilm (photoresist); Air Liquide, Linde (gases)
- OSAT: In-house (EMIB, Foveros), Amkor (strategic partnership for EMIB), ASE
