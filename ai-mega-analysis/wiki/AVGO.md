## Moat Score: 4
- Deep networking/infrastructure IP moat with proprietary SerDes, PHY, and custom ASIC capabilities; VMware acquisition adds enterprise software recurring revenue; custom ASIC business for hyperscalers (Google TPU, Meta) creates sticky relationships. Diversified across networking, broadband, wireless, and software.

- Equipment: ASML, Applied Materials, Lam Research, TEL, KLA (all via TSMC)
- Materials: SUMCO, GlobalWafers, Shin-Etsu, JSR (all via TSMC)
- OSAT: TSMC (CoWoS — flagged as constraint), ASE, Amkor; developing proprietary 3.5D F2F packaging

## Dhandho Signal
- $19B FCF on $3B CapEx is exceptional capital efficiency; 65% GM reflects pricing power. However, $53B net debt from VMware acquisition is significant (Net Debt/EBITDA 1.8x). P/E 113 reflects FY2024 depressed earnings from VMware integration; FY2026E $24B net income implies forward P/E ~85. AI custom ASIC growth is real but CoWoS-constrained. Wait for debt paydown and earnings normalization.
