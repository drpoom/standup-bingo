## Growth (CAGR 2024-2026E)

## Moat Score: 3
- Proprietary BCD process technology co-developed with foundries; strong power management IC design capability; fabless model enables asset-light growth; but analog power management is competitive (TI, Infineon, onsemi) and switching costs are moderate

## Key Suppliers
- Foundry: TSMC (primary BCD process), Vanguard International Semiconductor (VIS — strategic diversification agreement)
- Equipment: Applied Materials, Lam Research, KLA, ASML (purchased by foundries, not directly)
- Materials: Shin-Etsu, SUMCO, GlobalWafers, Siltronic (via foundries); JSR, TOK, Fujifilm (photoresist via foundries)
- OSAT: ASE Group (primary), Amkor, JCET — standard packaging (QFN, BGA, CSP)

## Dhandho Signal
- Extreme growth premium (P/E 158, EV/EBITDA 113) prices in flawless execution of 26% CAGR. Any growth deceleration will punish the stock severely. Fabless model is asset-light but creates TSMC concentration risk. At these multiples, this is a momentum/growth play, not a value investment. Wait for meaningful correction or earnings miss to establish position.
