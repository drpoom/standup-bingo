## Growth (CAGR 2024-2026E)

## Moat Score: 5
- CUDA software ecosystem lock-in creates unassailable developer network effects; dominant AI training/inference GPU market share (~80%+); no credible alternative ecosystem for large-scale AI workloads.

## Key Suppliers
- Foundry: TSMC (primary, ~90%+ of advanced chips on 4nm/5nm/3nm)
- Equipment: ASML, Applied Materials, Lam Research, TEL, KLA (all via TSMC)
- Materials: SUMCO, Shin-Etsu, JSR (all via TSMC)
- OSAT: TSMC (CoWoS — critical bottleneck), ASE, Amkor

## Dhandho Signal
- Peak-cycle AI demand driving 65% revenue CAGR; valuation at P/E 176 and EV/EBITDA 63 prices in sustained hypergrowth. CoWoS/HBM supply constraints are the gating factor. Cyclical risk: any demand slowdown would compress margins and multiples violently. Net cash position ($21.5B) provides downside cushion but valuation leaves no margin of safety.
