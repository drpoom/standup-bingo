# Dhandho Scoring Matrix — All 67 Companies

**Date:** 2026-05-11  
**Methodology:** Composite score = weighted average of 7 Dhandho criteria (equal weight) + adjustments for moat quality, AI tailwind strength, and balance sheet fortress score.

## Scoring Methodology

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Low Valuation | 1/7 | P/E, EV/EBITDA, EV/FCF relative to growth |
| High Growth | 1/7 | Revenue CAGR, earnings growth trajectory |
| Misunderstood Cyclicality | 1/7 | Is the market mispricing cyclical positioning? |
| Margin of Safety | 1/7 | Downside protection from valuation, assets, cash |
| Balance Sheet | 1/7 | Net debt/EBITDA, cash position, financial fortress |
| Moat | 1/7 | Competitive advantage durability (1-5 scale) |
| AI Tailwind | 1/7 | Direct/indirect AI revenue exposure and quality |

**Munger Filters (elimination criteria):**
- P/E > 50x (TTM or 2026E) → ELIMINATE
- Negative FCF → ELIMINATE
- Net Debt/EBITDA > 4x → ELIMINATE
- Moat < 3/5 → ELIMINATE (unless exceptional value)
