## Key Observations

1. **Valuation dispersion is extreme:** Palantir trades at ~190x GAAP P/E while UiPath trades at ~14x EV/FCF. Alibaba is the cheapest at ~10-12x EV/FCF.

2. **AI exposure varies widely:** C3.ai and Palantir are pure AI plays; Apellis has essentially no AI exposure (biopharma). Oracle and Snowflake have high AI exposure through infrastructure/data workloads.

3. **Growth vs. Profitability tradeoff:** Palantir and Snowflake show high growth with improving margins. UiPath and MongoDB show decelerating growth. Oracle is a mature cash machine with accelerating cloud growth.

4. **Apellis is miscategorized:** APLS is a biopharma company, not cloud/software. It should likely be moved to a healthcare/biotech batch.

5. **FCF generation leaders:** Oracle ($17.5B), Alibaba ($20B+), Palantir ($2.27B at 51% margin), and Datadog ($915M) are strong FCF generators. C3.ai and Apellis are still FCF-negative.

6. **Rule of 40 standings:** Palantir (127%) >> Datadog (~50%) > Snowflake (~36%) > MongoDB (~34%) > UiPath (~26%) > Oracle (~52% with growth) > C3.ai (loss-making) > Apellis (N/A).
