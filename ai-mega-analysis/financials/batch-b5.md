## Key Themes Across Batch

1. **AI/Data Center Power Demand is the Dominant Theme:** Every company in this batch is directly or indirectly benefiting from the AI infrastructure buildout. VST and CEG provide baseload nuclear/gas power; GEV manufactures generation and grid equipment; ETN provides electrical distribution; VRT delivers data center cooling and power systems; SMID supplies underground utility vaults.

2. **Nuclear Renaissance:** VST and CEG are the two largest competitive nuclear operators. Nuclear PTCs and data center PPAs are driving record profitability. CEG's Calpine acquisition will add gas generation.

3. **Electrification & Grid Investment:** GEV's Electrification segment (+18% organic revenue) and ETN's Electrical Americas (+13% revenue) are seeing massive order growth driven by data center power distribution needs.

4. **Vertiv is the Purest Play:** VRT's 17.5% organic growth, 19.4% adjusted margin, and ~30% TTM order growth make it the most direct beneficiary of AI data center buildout in this batch.

5. **Small-Cap Leverage:** SMID, while tiny ($78.5M revenue), shows how AI infrastructure demand cascades down to even precast concrete suppliers — 171% utility product growth.

6. **Margin Expansion Trend:** Every company showed significant margin improvement in FY2024, driven by operating leverage from volume growth in AI-related segments.
