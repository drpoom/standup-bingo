### Key Observations

1. **AI Infrastructure Winners:** ANET, MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, META are the primary AI infrastructure beneficiaries with massive capex cycles driving their own and others' growth.

2. **Highest AI Purity:** PLTR and AI (C3.ai) are the purest AI software plays, but PLTR has vastly superior unit economics (44% FCF margin vs. C3.ai's -11%).

3. **Most Profitable AI Monetization:** APP (AppLovin) has the most impressive AI-driven profitability story — 58% Adjusted EBITDA margin with 43% revenue growth, all powered by its AXON AI engine.

4. **Data Foundation for AI:** SNOW and MDB are positioning as essential data infrastructure for AI workloads, but face growth deceleration concerns.

5. **Observability for AI:** DDOG is the critical monitoring layer for AI infrastructure, with strong FCF margins (~27%).

6. **Networking for AI:** ANET and CIEN are the picks-and-shovels plays for AI data center networking, with ANET having superior margins and growth.

7. **Valuation Extremes:** PLTR trades at ~200x earnings (priced for perfection), while 9988.HK trades at ~12x (discounted for China risk). APP offers the best growth+profitability combination at ~30x P/E.
