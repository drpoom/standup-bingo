## Key Strategic Developments

### Lidar Business
- **Mercedes-Benz L3 Partnership:** Confirmed as strategic lidar partner and supplier for Mercedes-Benz L3 autonomy models (Europe + China), with production at new Galileo manufacturing center in Thailand
- **Market Share:** #1 globally in long-range ADAS lidar with 43% share in 2025 (Yole Group); 55% share in China in March 2026 (Gasgoo) — ~3x the #2 player
- **GAC Toyota bZ3X:** ATX lidar selected — first entry into Japanese automotive ecosystem
- **Beijing Auto Show:** Featured across 56 vehicle models from 24 brands, #1 in lidar presence
- **Li Auto:** First mass production deployment of FTX blind-spot lidar (up to 4 lidars per vehicle)
- **Xiaomi:** New design wins for overseas models, SOP from 2027
- **Cadillac VISTIQ:** Industry's first mass-produced in-cabin lidar
- **Robotics:** Zelos exclusive 200K unit design win; Neolix largest supplier; NIU e-scooter entry; Honor humanoid robot "Lightning" championship

### Technology Breakthroughs
- **Picasso SPAD-SoC:** World's first 6D full-color ultra-sensitive lidar SPAD-SoC — integrates RGB + XYZ at chip level, >40% PDE
- **ETX Lidar:** 600m range, up to 4,320 channels, SOP 2H 2026, broad adoption expected 2027-2028
- **KargoBot Space 2.0:** First mass-production deployment of 6D full-color ETX

### Strategic Growth Initiatives (SGI)
- **Kosmo:** First SGI product — spatial intelligence device for 3D data infrastructure
- Early orders received; recurring revenue model (hardware + AI software + spatial data + platform services)
- Targeting robotics simulation, immersive media, 4D entertainment

### New Reporting Structure
- **Segment 1: Lidar Business** — self-sustained profitability (~RMB 42M operating profit in Q1)
- **Segment 2: Strategic Growth Initiatives (SGI)** — long-term upside, ~RMB 100M revenue expected in FY2026
